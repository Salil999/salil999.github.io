Who I Am

Hey there! My name's Shashank Saxena (or more commonly known as Shank). I gradated from University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign with a degree in computer engineering. I currently work at Atlassian - helping them up the status in Statuspage! Aside from work, I'm usually hanging out with my friends over some bubble tea or ping pong and I really really like sushi. It's certainly going to be on the rise in the next few years with the vast amount of data that is being produced daily.
